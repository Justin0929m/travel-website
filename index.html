Advantages

The world is a beautiful and diverse place, filled with natural wonders, stunning landscapes, and unique cultures. From the majestic mountains to the sparkling oceans, there are countless breathtaking sights to behold. The world is also home to an incredible array of flora and fauna, from towering trees to colorful birds and exotic animals. Human-made structures such as intricate architecture, vibrant street art, and impressive monuments also add to the beauty of the world. The world's beauty can inspire a sense of wonder and appreciation for the natural and cultural world, reminding us of the vast and extraordinary planet we inhabit.

Ireland

Ireland is off the northwest of Europe and west of Great Britain. It is divided into two parts: the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland, which is part of the United Kingdom. Ireland is a member of the European Union.

Toronto

Toronto is Canada's largest city and a world leader in business, finance, technology, entertainment and culture. Its large population of immigrants has made it one of the most multicultural cities in the world.

England

England is part of the United Kingdom and shares land borders with Wales and Scotland, with the Irish Sea to its northwest and the Celtic Sea to its southwest.

France

France is one of the oldest nations on Earth and the most ethnically diverse country in Europe, making it a world leader in nearly all aspects of culture, including cuisine, wine-making, politics, philosophy, music, art, film, fashion, literature, and sports.
